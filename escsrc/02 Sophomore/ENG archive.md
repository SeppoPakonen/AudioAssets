ABOUT THIS RELEASE
Sophomore collects songs I composed at age 15 in 2005 while living in Oulu, Finland (European Capital of Culture 2026). The pieces were born across the Finnish summer break before upper secondary school (lukio) and during my first study year. I wrote, arranged, and produced these works independently. Most originals survived as MP3 demos; during the 2020 pandemic I began transcribing them into proper notation to preserve how they were first conceived and to document the ideas behind the original project names that informed today’s lyrics.

The year also included forming a band, “The Vice,” with three classmates from the Oulu Steiner School (1989 cohort) plus one external guitarist. We learned to rehearse as an ensemble and worked through early stage fright together. Personally, I grew up feeling little support from my local environment, so I built bands and projects on my own and focused on songwriting rather than performing live.
