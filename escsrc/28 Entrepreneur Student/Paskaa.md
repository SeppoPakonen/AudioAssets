## Tyyli
### Alkuperäinen
```
Acoustic folk rock song in a minor key at a moderate tempo.
The instrumentation features a single acoustic guitar playing a fingerpicked arpeggiated pattern throughout the song.
The guitar provides both rhythmic and harmonic accompaniment.
The male vocalist sings in a clear, slightly melancholic baritone, with a conversational delivery.
The song structure is verse-chorus with no bridge or instrumental break.
The melody is simple and follows the chord changes closely.
The production is clean with a natural acoustic sound, minimal reverb on the vocals and guitar, and no other effects.
The overall feel is intimate and reflective.
```
