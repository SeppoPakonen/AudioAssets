Title: Tuulen ratsastajan tarina
Date: 1.5.2024
Author: Seppo Pakonen

Typecast: confident/empowered
Content: chase your dreams
Idea: The lyrics celebrate the thrill of speeding down the open road, with each verse exploring the freedom and joy that comes with driving. Choruses are anthemic, echoing the idea of escaping the mundane and chasing adventures, making it a perfect fit for a high-end car campaign.

Structure idea: I,V1,PC1,C1,V2,PC2,C1,C2,B1,B2,C1,C2,O
