Description of the song:
This song is about finding peace and solace in a serene place, such as an island. It speaks of the wisdom of the island, and how its golden gates let in only good things. It encourages us to be kind to one another and to breathe in the same gentleness. The song also touches on the struggle of trying to find solace in a chaotic world, and the desire to escape the fear and darkness that comes with it. The song is a reminder to look for the good in every situation, and to remember to be kind to ourselves and to others.
