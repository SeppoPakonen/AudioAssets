Verse 1
I'm sorry for ignoring you, life gave me no time,
I felt like the two of us were just too busy and distant.
I miss feeling like I was loved, going out for an evening and searching for restaurants and sceneries for enjoyment,
I miss the way you smiled and hugged me for comfort.
I promised to love you always, but life kept me busy,
time goes faster than I can solve problems with you.
I go on trips that keep me away from you, I miss the kisses I used to get with you, my feelings are gone as life pulls me away,
but time stands still when I'm with you.
When two lovers meet like me and my romantic partner, it gets quieter, the environment dims as we move forward and the sky darkens as the night takes over,
until the last touch of my romantic partner, after which we fall asleep.
Me and my romantic partner,
we haven't tried hard enough to keep the bond alive.
I thank my romantic partner for taking the time to ask for forgiveness and to forgive myself.
Maybe we'll meet again if I make the effort, good luck baby, wherever you go in life.

Verse 2
In this new place, questions arise when I think about the lack of my romantic partner, without a name I forget who I am,
it doesn't just remain in forgetting the name, but everything else about my romantic partner is forgotten.
I'm traveling around the world trying to forget, looking into the eyes of a possible future wife as I try to distract myself from my old romantic partner.
I'm trying to make the feelings stop by running away, our eyes meet and I think I fell in love, but I'm scared of the pain.
I'm searching for an answer in my mind, but there's nothing there.
I'm afraid of feeling, of falling in love, but the fact remains that I'll be disappointed sometimes as I try to move on.
I'm telling my romantic partner not to wait until tomorrow, because every day brings us closer to happiness as life keeps going.
In contrast, let the emotions flow together with love, as I remain connected.
One might wonder what is the name of my romantic partner, she won't know, I'm not telling her, but she'll notice when we're together,
if she feels the same way as me.
