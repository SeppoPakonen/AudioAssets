Meta:

Joyful Anthem: The lyrics center around a vibrant summer get-together where everyone is dancing without a care. The message revolves around seizing the moment, embracing spontaneity, and enjoying the exhilaration of youth. As the night progresses, however, the song subtly transitions into reflections on how these perfect moments are fleeting, and the coming of dawn brings back reality, emphasizing the importance of living in the now.
EDM, Pop, 118bpm, mixed vocals, catchy synth hooks, upbeat drum patterns
